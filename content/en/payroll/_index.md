## Payroll with CasaCare

### Legal hiring made simple – powered by quitt.ch

Do you want to legally hire a nanny, cleaner, or household staff member but worry about doing it correctly?

Are you tired of wasting precious time filling in endless paperwork?

Do you fear the bureaucratic hassle of Swiss compliance?

If your answer is yes, CasaCare has the solution. Through our partnership with **quitt.ch**, we make hiring household staff effortless and fully compliant with Swiss legal requirements.

## Why CasaCare + quitt.ch?

With just a few clicks, you can hire your nanny or household staff member legally and fairly. No more chasing authorities or struggling with forms – quitt.ch takes care of everything for you.

### Your benefits:

- Complete registration of your employee with Swiss authorities
- Payroll accounting made easy
- Social security and insurance contributions handled automatically
- Monthly pay slips and annual wage statements provided
- Peace of mind that your staff are hired correctly and transparently

## Get started now

1. **Create your CasaCare quitt.ch account** by entering your details and those of your employee.

2. **Define the salary and hours** worked per month.

3. **Top up your quitt.ch account** via wire transfer – just like a pay-as-you-go mobile.

4. **Done!**

From then on, quitt.ch will manage all the paperwork, pay the mandatory contributions, and provide you with all essential documents – including the employment contract, monthly pay slips, and wage statements.
