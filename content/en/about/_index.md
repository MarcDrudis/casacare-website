## Introducing CasaCare

Entrusted with placing dedicated domestic staff into households across Zurich, CasaCare specialises in providing highly qualified nannies, cleaning professionals, and private chefs.
CasaCare was created with a clear mission: to bring trust, warmth, and excellence into the heart of every home.

We believe that a household is more than a physical space — it is a place where wellbeing, balance, and emotional comfort should naturally flourish. Our services are designed to support families in creating a safe, stress-free, and harmonious home environment, offering consistency, reliability, and a holistic approach to care.

At CasaCare, we value trust and safety, human connection, attention to detail, cultural sensitivity, stability, and continuity. Above all, we want families to experience a genuine sense of relief, comfort, and confidence in the people who support their daily lives.

## Our Approach

As a domestic staff placement agency, CasaCare tailors every recruitment process to the unique needs of each client.
We begin with an introductory meeting to understand the household, the expectations, and the exact profile required. Together, we define the role, responsibilities, and employment conditions.

From there, your dedicated CasaCare consultant begins the search for the ideal candidate. Through a rigorous application process and preliminary interviews, we ensure that only the most suitable and carefully vetted professionals are presented for consideration.

Throughout the entire recruitment journey, both clients and candidates remain fully informed and in continuous communication with their consultant. This transparent and personalised approach allows us to build long-term relationships based on trust, discretion, and exceptional service.
